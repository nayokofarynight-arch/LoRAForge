Background
Balirossa is a female knight from Klyrode, she graduated from a Knight Academy and is currently an adventurer. Until she met Flio and Rys, she was in a party with Blossom, Byleri and Belano. Her family was once noble but has lost their status.

Personality
Balirossa doesn't seem that worldly at first, seemingly having never experienced a fight against magic beasts; while training against a bear Flio used as her sparring partner, she stupidly believed it would obey the rules of chivalry.

She utterly fears Gholl the demon king, trying her damnedest to stay away from him; ironically her chivalry had endeared her to him when they first met. Balirossa once fainted with eyes open from just the shock of his demonic aura.

Balirossa is a bit selfish in wanting to regain nobility; becoming distraught that Blossom got the coveted title of dragon slayer, as that ALONE would qualify one for noble status. Ironically, she eventually accepts Gholl's affection and ends up married to the former demon king; Balirossa would become one of the queens when Gholl takes back the throne.
