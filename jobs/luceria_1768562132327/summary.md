Appearance
Luceria is roughly the same height as Sylphy. She has dark and silver hair tied into twin ponytails which reaches the top of her thighs, pointed elfin ears, black bat-like wings and a black demon tail. She wears a lolita style black and red dress with pastel red stockings with white diamonds below her knees, and black boots decorated with red patterns.

Personality and traits
In contrast to Sylphy, Luceria is more blunt in her speech and mannerisms, openly demanding monster cores from Kaito in a rude tone, although she ascribes much of her rudeness to it being the way demons are and to take no notice of it. Despite which, she enjoys spending time with him, to the point where she will ask where he has been rather than being with her and Sylphy. When Kaito mentioned that he wanted to form a party with other adventurers, Luceria said there was no need to, and that the three of them were enough on their own. When Kaito showed off various items he purchased, she found flaws in them and soon pulled Sylphy away, who praised their master's choices. She acts very much like a tsundere around him and others, secretly enjoying the attention she gets. She questioned why Sylphy was known as 'Syl' and demanded a nickname of her own, and although she commented that 'Luce' was not creative, accepted it as one nonetheless. In combat, she mainly uses "Hellfire of Destruction", a flamethrower-like attack fired from her staff, and "Corrosive Breath", a poison mist attack that corrodes the target on contact. Upon levelling up sufficiently, she also gains access to "Beauty of Gluttony", a more powerful form where she can deal more damage. She becomes aware of her appeal in this form, and is more demanding of compliments from Kaito before undoing the bond. Following another level up, she was able to use Purgatory's Passion, a very powerful attack. When Bellia was added to the group, she was initially somewhat condescending to him, but soon warmed up to him too.

Similarly to Sylphy, she has a fear of bugs, although she will not admit to it.

Abilities and Powers
As a viscount class demon, Luceria is very powerful, able to handle many opponents with relative ease. Unlike Sylphy, Luceria uses a staff to fire her main attack, Hellfire of Destruction, a powerful flamethrower attack that can do impressive damage to most of her foes. In addition, she can use Corrosive Breath to literally corrode an enemy into sludge, an attack Kaito dubbed as terrifying. Upon levelling up sufficiently, she became able to use Beauty of Gluttony, absorbing Kaito's HP to bolster her own strength and assume an adult form temporarily. Reversing this bond is something she can do at will, although she seems to enjoy teasing Kaito with the threat of keeping it for longer. Following her second level up, she gained the ability Purgatory's Passion, coincidentally at the same time as Sylphy.

Hellfire of Destruction: is Luceria's standard attack. She summons a dark staff with a red gem in it to cast a powerful flamethrower-like attack that will harm any caught in its' way, and can presumably be used to alter the environment as well, as when she was on an ice floor with Kaito, Sylphy and Bellia, she suggested melting the ice away.

Corrosive breath: is Luceria's second attack. A cloud of glowing red spots surrounds her and then engulfs her target, turning them to sludge. Kaito mentally noted the attack was terrifying to see, whereas Sylphy shuddered and soon covered her eyes, unable to watch.

Beauty of Gluttony: is the first ability Luceria acquired upon levelling up. It is a last resort attack, draining the health of her master to bolster her own and regain her adult form temporarily. In this form she can use a powerful attack called God-Killer Duststorm, something her foe stated she should not be able to use.

Purgatory's Passion: Luceria gains immense power, a glowing image of her older self shadowing her, as she flies at her foe and destroys them in a blaze of fire.
