Personality
Haruno is kind and gentle, and cares deeply for her comrades. She believes that human lives come first, and is willing to do anything which will further the Anti-Demon Corps mission of protecting humankind - even trying out 'Lending' with Kyouka Uzen's Slave power. Haruno also supported Yumeji Shimomura's decision to promote Kyouka to be her unit's Vice-Chief, despite being her senior.

As a veteran of the 7th Unit, Haruno is familiar with the current active-duty members, and is well-liked by all of them. She was especially close with Kyouka and Yumeji, and appreciates her time spent with them before being assigned to the Away Team.



Background
Haruno first enlisted in the Anti-Demon Corps to fulfil her goal of protecting those around her. After completing her basic training, she was assigned to the 7th Unit, with Yumeji as her Chief. She later welcomed Kyouka as a new member, after the latter joined the 7th Unit.

Haruno would later be reassigned to the Away Team, tasked with exploring Mato and collecting Mato Peaches for consumption in its most remote and dangerous areas.

Synopsis
Haruno returns to the 7th Unit to take charge while Kyouka attends the Commander's Election in the Shangri-La dimension. She notes how much neater the base has become since her reassignment, due to Yuuki fulfilling his duties as caretaker.

After the election, Haruno caught up with the other members of the 7th Unit, and became acquainted with Yuuki Wakura. She introduced him to her ability, Origami, which she had been honing during Kyouka's absence, and granted him some shikigami (familiars) to use around the base for chores.

Haruno later accompanied Kyouka and Yuuki into the field for a Shuuki extermination mission. She requested to borrow Kyouka's power, unlocking a new form in the process. After testing out its capabilities, Haruno mentioned that this could be used to heal Yumeji, who was recovering after her second duel with Kyouka.

Haruno later granted Yuuki a reward, cleaning his ears for him, kissing him, and allowing him to nurse from her breasts. She expressed gratitude to Yuuki for granting Kyouka some comfort in her role as Chief of her Unit, from all of the troubles she had to handle. When asked about her own source of comfort, Haruno said that being able to see everyone's smiling faces was her source of comfort.

Powers and Abilities
